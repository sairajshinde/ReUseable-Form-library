Scholarship Scheme for Employees Children

"TOI Education Excellence Awards" is a Scholarship Scheme for Employees Children. It is aimed to encourage and reward academic excellence amongst the children of our Employees' and guide them towards rewarding careers.

You can nominate your child / children for Scholarship under one of the following categories:

  • Post Graduate Studies (for those who completed Graduation)Â 
  • Graduate Studies (for those who have passed XII standard)
  • Undergraduate Studies (for those who have passed X standard)
  • For students with special needs
  • For National/State-level Achievers

Please note: The child should have passed X / XII / Graduation in the same year in which the application for scholarship is submitted.
