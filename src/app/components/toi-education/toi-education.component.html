Scholarship Scheme for Employees Children

"TOI Education Excellence Awards" is a Scholarship Scheme for Employees Children. It is aimed to encourage and reward our employees' talented children and motivate them to perform their best in their educational pursuits.

You can apply for a Scholarship for your child under any of the following categories:

  • Post Graduate Studies (for graduates)
  • Graduate Studies (For Std XII students)
  • Undergraduate Studies (For Std X students)
  • For specially-abled students
  • For National/State-level Achievers

This online version is designed to enable you to submit your Child's Scholarship Application.
