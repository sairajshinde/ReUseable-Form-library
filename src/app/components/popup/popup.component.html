Process Flow

1. New Application

Steps:

APPLY ON TIMESCAPE

  • Log in to the Timescape and navigate to myHR / ESSENTIALS -> TOI Education Excellence Awards
  • Go to "New Application", enter the Child's information and upload all the supporting documents

REVIEW

  • Upon review of all applications, result would be announced via HR Connect.

RESULT ANNOUNCEMENT

  • Employees of the selected awardees will be able to download the Scholarship Certificate from timescape
  • A hard copy of the certificate will be sent to the HR representative at your location.

DISBURSEMENT

  • For disbursement of the Scholarship Amount, employees are requested to upload a cancelled cheque in the child’s name. .
  • In case your child does not have a bank account, we request you to kindly open one to enable timely transfer.

2. Scholarship Continuation (Next Academic Year)

Steps:

APPLY ON TIMESCAPE

  • Log in to the Timescape and navigate to myHR / ESSENTIALS ->TOI Education Excellence Awards

GO TO "CHECK STATUS"

  • Go to "Check Status", and click on Continue Scholarship. Enter the marks / CGPA and upload the supporting documents

REVIEW

  • Continuation of Scholarship is subject to meeting the minimum academic performance criteria.

VERIFICATION AND DISBURSEMENT

  • Verify child’s bank account details. In case of a change in bank account, please upload a new cancelled cheque in the child’s name
