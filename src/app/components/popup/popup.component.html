Process Flow

1. New Application

Step 1:

  • Log in to the Timescape and navigate to myHR / ESSENTIALS -> TOI Education Excellence Awards

Step 2:

  • Go to "New Application", enter the Child's information and upload all the supporting documents
  • Upon review of all applications, result would be announced via HR Connect. Employees of the selected awardees will be able to download the Scholarship Certificate from timescape. A hard copy of the certificate will be sent to the HR representative at your location.
  • For disbursement of the Scholarship Amount, employees are requested to upload a cancelled cheque in the child’s name. In case your child does not have a bank account, we request you to kindly open one to enable timely transfer..

2. Continuity Application

Step 1:

  • Log in to the Timescape and navigate to myHR / ESSENTIALS ->TOI Education Excellence Awards

Step 2:

  • Go to "Check Status", and click on Continue Scholarship. Enter the marks / CGPA and upload the supporting documents.

Step 3:

  • Upon confirmation, you would be able to download the certificate from timescape.

Step 4:

  • Please verify child’s bank account details. In case of change, please upload a new cancelled cheque in the child’s name

Continuation of Scholarship is subject to meeting the minimum academic performance criteria.
