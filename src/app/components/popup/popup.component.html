Process Flow

1. New TOI Education Excellence Scholarship Application

Steps:

APPLY ON TIMESCAPE

  • Log in to the Timescape.
  • Complete the Scholarship Application Form.
  • Upload required documents (e.g. previous year’s marksheet, school/college ID proof).

HR REVIEW

  • HR will evaluate all applications for eligibility and completeness.

RESULT ANNOUNCEMENT

  • HR will publish the final list of selected candidates on Timescape.
  • Employee must submit a cancelled cheque in the child’s name.
  • HR will share beneficiary details with the Finance department.

DISBURSEMENT

  • The scholarship amount will be transferred to the student/child’s bank account.

2. Scholarship Continuation (Next Academic Year)

Steps:

SUBMIT UPDATED DOCUMENTS

  • Upload the latest academic marksheets via the Timescape.

HR REVIEW

  • HR will assess the continuation eligibility based on academic performance.

BANK ACCOUNT CONFIRMATION

  • Employee must confirm or update the child’s bank account details.

DISBURSEMENT

  • Finance will credit the scholarship amount to the student’s bank account.
